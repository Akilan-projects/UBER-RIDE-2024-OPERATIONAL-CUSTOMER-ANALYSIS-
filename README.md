                                          🚖 Uber Ride 2024 – Operational & Customer Analysis
**📊 Project Overview**

This project analyzes Uber ride data from 2024 to evaluate booking trends, operational performance, customer behavior, and ride completion efficiency. The analysis aims to identify patterns that influence cancellations, ride reliability, and customer retention.

The dataset shows strong overall demand with 150K total bookings and 93K completed rides. Despite high service ratings from both drivers and customers, operational challenges such as cancellations and low repeat customer rates highlight opportunities for improvement.

**🎯 Business Objectives**

- Understand ride demand and booking patterns

- Identify top vehicle types driving revenue

- Analyze cancellation and incomplete ride reasons

- Evaluate operational performance using VTAT and CTAT

- Examine customer retention and repeat usage

**📊 Dashboard Insights**

**1️⃣ Revenue by Vehicle Type**

Auto, Go Mini, and Go Sedan generate the highest revenue and ride volume. These categories represent the core demand for ride-hailing services.

Premium vehicle types such as Premier Sedan and Uber XL contribute smaller but strategic revenue segments.

**2️⃣ Monthly Booking Trends**

Booking patterns remain relatively stable throughout the year, indicating consistent demand with minimal seasonal fluctuations.

**3️⃣ Ride Cancellation Analysis**

A significant portion of rides are cancelled due to operational and coordination issues.

**Customer Cancellation Reasons**

- Incorrect pickup address

- Change of travel plans

- Driver not moving toward pickup

- Drivers requesting cancellation

**Driver Cancellation Reasons**

- Passenger-related issues

- Health concerns

- Vehicle problems

- Passenger limit violations

These cancellations reduce operational efficiency and impact user experience.

**4️⃣ Operational Efficiency (VTAT vs CTAT)**

**VTAT – Vehicle Turnaround Time**
Measures how quickly a vehicle becomes available for the next ride.

**CTAT – Customer Turnaround Time**
Measures the total time from booking to ride completion.

The analysis shows that vehicle availability is improving, but customers still experience delays due to pickup coordination issues and ride cancellations.

**5️⃣ Customer Retention Insights**

Repeated customers represent only 1,206 users, which is extremely low compared to the total number of bookings.

This indicates that the platform depends heavily on new or occasional users rather than loyal customers.

Improving ride reliability, reducing cancellations, and implementing loyalty programs could significantly increase repeat usage.
